Caveman.Widget.Form - Widget for generating 'form'.

SYNOPSIS

(defvar form (make-form  
              :method :POST  
              :action "/post"  
              :components other-widgets))  
(add form widget)  
(render form)

EXTERNAL SYMBOLS

  • Class: <caveman-widget-form> inherits (<caveman-widget>)
    action Accessor: form-action
    method Accessor: form-method
    components Accessor: form-components
  • Method: initialize-instance [(this <caveman-widget-form>) &rest initargs]
  • Function: make-form [&rest args] 
      A synonim for `(make-instance ' ...)`.
  • Method: add [(this <caveman-widget-form>) widget] 
      Add another widget to this form. That will be rendered in 'form' tag.
  • Method: validate [(this <caveman-widget-form>) values]
