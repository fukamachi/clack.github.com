Welcome to Clack!

      (clackup
      (lambda (env)
      '(200
      (:content-type "text/plain")
      ("Hello, Clack!"))))
Open your web browser and go to http://localhost:5000/.

Easy to Setup

Install Quicklisp and use: 
      (ql:quickload :clack)
